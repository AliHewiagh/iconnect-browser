About us

Papervideo’s mission is to provide all students across South Africa with their own, personal extra teacher. We do this by embedding QR codes into our Exam Paper Books and Subjects Maps. The QR codes link to video lessons that specifically cover the question or topic that you are struggling with. Using this app, you can scan these QR codes and get instant access to these videos. With over 10 000 videos across grades 8 to 12, for Mathematics, Physical Sciences, Life Sciences, Natural Sciences & Accounting, you’ll have access to the largest collection of video resources in South Africa, taught some of South Africa’s best teachers.
